Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 30, 2025

Zero data collection by default

This website is designed to collect as little information as possible. By default, we do not track you, set analytics cookies, or collect usage data. We believe privacy is a baseline — not an option.

Contact form and submitted information

The contact form on this site is the only feature that intentionally collects data. When you submit the form you provide the following fields:

  • Name (required)
  • Email (required) — used only to reply to your inquiry
  • Project title (optional)
  • Target iOS version, devices, message (optional)

How submissions are stored

Each submission is saved as a JSON file in an Azure Blob Storage container. Files are created only when you submit the form. The site does not forward or share these submissions with third parties automatically.

Data retention

Submissions are retained until manually deleted. If you'd like your submission removed, contact the site owner at the email address provided in your submission or open an issue in this repository.

No analytics, no tracking

This site does not use analytics tools, trackers, or advertising networks. Nothing on this website is used to build a profile about you.

Security

Access to stored submissions is protected by the storage account's credentials. The Azure Storage connection string is kept secret in the application settings and is not published in this repository.

Legal and contact

If you have questions about your data or want your submission deleted, contact the site owner at the email you supplied when you submitted the form.

By using the contact form you agree to the terms described in this privacy policy.
